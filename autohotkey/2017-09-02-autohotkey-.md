Hotkey syntax:

| Key       | 
|-----------|--------------------------|
| ^
| #
| +
| !
| <#
| #>
| <^>!
|-----------|--------------------------|
{: .table-code}

^ = Control
# = Win
+ = Shift
! = Alt
<# = Left Win
#> = Right Win
<^>! = AltGr

; Prefix a hotkey with ~ to not block the native function
; Numpad0 & Numpad2 = When pressed together
